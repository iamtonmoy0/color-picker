🔒 Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters.

Last Updated: July 2026

Overview

Color Picker Pro is designed with privacy as a core principle. The extension operates entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information.

Information We Collect

Color Picker Pro does not collect any personal information.

  • No names
  • No email addresses
  • No passwords
  • No browsing history
  • No location information
  • No analytics or tracking data

Color History

When you select a color, the HEX value is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This allows the extension to remember previously selected colors.

This information never leaves your device.

Clipboard Access

After selecting a color, the extension automatically copies the HEX color code to your clipboard for your convenience.

Clipboard data is never transmitted or stored externally.

Permissions

The extension requests only the permissions required for its functionality.

  • storage — Save your color history locally.

Third-Party Services

Color Picker Pro does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, tracking scripts, or external servers.

Data Security

Since all information remains on your device, your data stays under your control.

Children's Privacy

The extension is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when necessary. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the extension, please contact us through the project's GitHub repository or Chrome Web Store listing.
